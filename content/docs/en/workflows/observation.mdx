---
title: Observation
description: Inspect agent communication, context, tool calls, and intermediate state.
---

Observation is the control plane for a multi-agent run.

You should be able to inspect:

- Agent-to-agent messages.
- Prompt composition.
- Mounted context.
- Tool calls.
- Intermediate outputs.
- Handoffs.

## Human intervention

When direction drifts, intervene at the exact agent that needs correction.
