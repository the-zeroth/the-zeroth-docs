---
title: Space Management
description: Create, switch, import, export, and share Definition Spaces.
---

You can have any number of Definition Spaces. Spaces are independent and do not contaminate each other. At any moment, only one Space is active for editing or running.

You can download Spaces from the official sharing platform or upload your own Space:
